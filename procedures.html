PLACEMENT PROCEDURE

Phase 1

1. The invitation, containing the relevant information and the Job Announcement Form (JAF), is sent to the companies/organizations (hereafter called organizations) through email/post.

2. Organizations fill the JAF and send it back to the Training and Placement Cell, NIT Manipur via email/post. It is mandatory to complete the JAF with due care as it serves as an introduction of the job profile for the candidates.

3. Suitable dates for Pre-Placement Talk (PPT) are decided as per mutual convenience.

4. The company visits NIT Manipur campus and conducts its PPT.

Phase 2

1. The Training and Placement Cell allots a date to the organization, which is based on a variety of parameters, but mostly depends on the interest shown by the company and the candidates.

2. The organization can confirm or negotiate the date with the placement cell.

Phase 3

1. Organizations visit the campus on the given date and conducts the various stages of placement.

2. An organization is required to furnish the final list of selected students as soon as possible after the final completion of procedure.
