INTERNSHIP

Internships/Industrial Training are known to be one of the most knowledgable experiences in an undergraduate or a management course. It provides opportunities for students to gain experience in their discipline, determine if they have an interest in a particular career, and create a network of contacts. And here at NIT Manipur, Internships are given due importance and also included as part of the curriculum for the pre-final year students. It provides the students with hands-on-training required to become a competent professional in the future. It is thus given a high priority at NIT Manipur and is compulsory of all the departments.




Students usually chose to go for internships in their 3rd year after the completion of their 6th semester of study. The companies also gain from the fresh perspective and inputs of the students and also bettering their profile among the general student community.
