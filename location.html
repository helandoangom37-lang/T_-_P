LOCATION OF NITM

NITM is located in Imphal district of Manipur.The temporary campus of NIT, Manipur is located at the campus of Government Polytechnic, Imphal which is only 1km from the permanent site. The temporary campus provides well furnished hostel accommodation with round the clock power supply, water supply and internet facilities. NIT, Manipur has a permanent site of 138.2 hectares in the lush green area of Langol in Imphal.
